ABOUT US

Kaffi Borgir is a family-run restaurant. We started in 2010 and have been going strong since then. The father Freddi is the manager with decades of prior experience in the restaurant business. Freddi’s oldest son Nonni has since overtaken the kitchen and is now teaching the youngest son Krissi the trade. The mother Halla takes care of the souvenir shop with her daughter Margrét.

We welcome you to our restaurant/cafe/souvenir shop and hope we can make your stay in the Mývatn area fulfilling!
